We're helping meet the world's demand for new doctors.

Join our 22,000 graduates including physicians, veterinarians, scientists, and public health and business professionals across the world.

Get to Know Our University

In addition to our School of Medicine we also have a class leading School of Veterinary Medicine, School of Arts and Sciences, and School of Graduate Studies.

990+ US residencies in 2022

Data as of May 2022. The largest provider of doctors into first-year US residencies for the last eight years.

As the medical school graduating the largest number of students annually, SGU places the largest number of graduates into residency programs each year, based on internal SGU graduate and residency placement data as of May 2022.
